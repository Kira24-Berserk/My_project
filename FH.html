Forza Horizon

Forza Horizon is an open-world racing video game series developed by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios. The series is a spin-off of the Forza Motorsport series and focuses on a more casual, arcade-style racing experience set in expansive open-world environments. The games feature a wide variety of cars, dynamic weather systems, and day-night cycles, allowing players to explore diverse landscapes while participating in various racing events and challenges. The Forza Horizon series has been praised for its stunning graphics, realistic driving mechanics, and extensive car customization options, making it a popular choice among racing game enthusiasts.

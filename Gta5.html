Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It is the fifth installment in the Grand Theft Auto series and was released for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in September 2013, followed by versions for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC in November 2014. The game features a single-player campaign with a story-driven plot involving three protagonists: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips. Players can also engage in online multiplayer mode, where they can participate in various activities such as heists, races, and combat.

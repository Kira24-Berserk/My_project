Pubg mobile

PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds (PUBG) is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed and published by PUBG Corporation, a subsidiary of South Korean video game company Bluehole. The game was initially released for Microsoft Windows via Steam's early access beta program in March 2017, followed by a full release in December 2017. PUBG has since been released on various platforms, including Xbox One, PlayStation 4, and mobile devices (iOS and Android).
